# Course Syllabus

### Linear Algebra
- Vectors, subspaces, linear independence
- Matmul, Matvec
- Inverse matrix, pseudo inverse matrix
- Determinant, rank
- Inner product, orthogonality
- Eigenvalues and eigenvectors
- SVD

### Calculus
- Limits and continuity
- Derivatives (product rule, chain rule)
- Integrals
- Partial derivatives, gradient, jacobian, hessian
- Matrix differentiation
- Optimization essentials: first-order and second-order conditions
